About Me

I am a dedicated M.Sc. student specializing in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, deeply passionate about translating complex theoretical models into practical, scalable solutions. My core focus lies in Deep Learning, particularly within Computer Vision and Large Language Models (LLMs).
